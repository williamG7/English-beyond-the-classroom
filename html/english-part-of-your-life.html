About the Presentation

Integrating English Without Pausing Your Life

To integrate English, we don't need to put our lives on hold. There are two main pathways to making it happen — and you can use both, depending on the day.

Integrated Practice

Utilizing the time already built into your daily routines — commuting, cleaning, grocery shopping. No extra hours required; just shift the language.

Dedicated Study

Setting aside exclusive, focused blocks of time during the week for grammar, vocabulary, or writing — treated with the same commitment as any other appointment.

The Golden Rule: Consistency matters more than the number of hours. A little English every day beats an intense session once a week.
Class Survey

What Does Our Group Look Like?

English classes & commuting habits
55.6% have never taken English classes. Most people (44.4%) spend 15–30 minutes commuting — perfect time for a podcast episode.
Screen time & writing preferences
38.9% watch 5 hours of video per week. Meanwhile 55.6% don't enjoy writing, suggesting audio and visual content as the best entry points.
Dedicated Study

The Focus Moment

The Commitment

If you decide to study 20 minutes a day, or half an hour three times a week, you must respect it as if it were a medical appointment. No excuses, no postponing.

Strategy for the Group

For those with 3–4 hours of free time daily

20 minutes of grammar or vocabulary every afternoon — short, consistent, and effective.

For those who only have the weekend

45-minute blocks on Saturday and Sunday mornings. Studying alone, as the majority prefers, keeps you focused.

The key principle

Find your window of time and protect it every week — whatever schedule works, stick to it.

Conclusion

Our Weekly Action Plan

Monday to Friday

  • Morning: 20 minutes of a podcast on the bus or train.
  • Afternoon / Evening: Entertainment in the original version with subtitles — series, Twitch, YouTube.

Weekend

  • 30 minutes of focused study (solo) — grammar, vocabulary, or writing.
  • Playing video games in English — full immersion without it feeling like studying.
