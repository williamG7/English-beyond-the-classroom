Practice Listening

Listening is one of the most important skills when learning English. With AI you can practise anytime, anywhere — no teacher needed. AI can speak to you, tell stories, have conversations and even test your understanding. You control the speed, the topic and the difficulty.

Recommended Apps

AI Tools for Listening

These are the best AI-powered apps to train your ear anytime, anywhere — no classroom needed.

Claude / ChatGPT

Generate stories, conversations and dictation exercises on any topic, at any level.

Speak App

Practise real spoken English with an AI conversation partner that gives instant feedback.

ELSA Speak

AI-powered feedback on pronunciation and listening comprehension, tailored to your accent.

How to Practice

Key Listening Strategies

Don't just hear — actively listen. These six techniques will sharpen your ear faster than passive listening ever could.

Active Listening

Focus on every word. Take notes while you listen. Don't just hear — understand.

Shadowing

Repeat what you hear immediately out loud. Great for pronunciation too.

Dictation

Write exactly what the AI says. Then compare and find your mistakes.

Comprehensible Input

Listen to content just one level above yours — not too easy, not too hard.

Accent Exposure

Listen to American, British and Australian English. Real life has many accents.

Predictive Listening

Guess what comes next before the AI continues. Keeps your brain active.

Daily Routine

20-Minute Daily Listening Plan

Spend just 20 minutes a day and you will notice the difference within weeks. Each activity targets a different listening skill.

Time Activity How to do it
5 min AI Story Ask AI for a short story. Listen, then answer 3 comprehension questions.
5 min Dictation AI reads a paragraph. You write what you hear.
5 min Voice Conversation Talk with AI — focus on understanding, not just answering.
5 min Shadowing AI reads sentences one by one. You repeat out loud immediately after.
Long-Term Progress

8-Week Progression Plan

Gradually increase the difficulty as your ear adapts. Start slow — finish at native speed.

Week Focus Level
Weeks 1–2 Everyday vocabulary Slow, neutral accent
Weeks 3–4 Casual conversations Normal speed
Weeks 5–6 Different accents American vs British
Weeks 7–8 Real content Native speed — YouTube, podcasts
Ready-to-Use

AI Prompts for Listening

Copy and paste any of these prompts into Claude, ChatGPT or any AI chat to start practising right away.

1

"Tell me a short story in simple English. Speak slowly. After finishing, ask me 3 comprehension questions."

2

"Read one sentence at a time for shadowing practice. Wait for me to say 'next' before you continue."

3

"Read a short paragraph for dictation. Pause between each sentence."

4

"Let's have a simple conversation in English about daily life. Use basic vocabulary and correct me if I make mistakes."

5

"Tell me the same story three times: once slowly, once at normal speed, once fast."
