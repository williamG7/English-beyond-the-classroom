Learn English with AI

Aprender inglés con inteligencia artificial es una forma revolucionaria y personalizada de mejorar el idioma. En esta sección nos enfocaremos en tres áreas clave: Listening, Reading y Writing.

Part 1: Practice Listening

Introduction

Listening is one of the most important skills when learning English. With Artificial Intelligence, you can practice anytime, anywhere — no teacher needed. AI can speak to you, tell stories, have conversations, and even test your understanding. The best part? You control the speed, the topic, and the difficulty. This section shows you how to use AI tools to seriously improve your listening skills.

Key Strategies & Resources - AI Tools

  • Claude / ChatGPT: Generate stories, conversations and dictation exercises.
  • Speak App: Elsa Speak
  • ELSA Speak: AI feedback on pronunciation and listening.

Study Plan: 20-Minute Daily Listening Plan

Time Activity How
5 min AI Story Ask AI for a short story. Listen, then answer 3 questions
5 min Dictation AI reads a paragraph. You write what you hear
5 min Voice Conversation Talk with AI — focus on understanding, not just answering
5 min Shadowing AI reads sentences one by one. You repeat out loud

Weekly Progression:

Week Focus Level
1–2 Everyday vocabulary Slow, neutral accent
3–4 Casual conversations Normal speed
5–6 Different accents American vs British
7–8 Real content Native speed (YouTube, podcasts)

Prompts to use: Ready-to-Use AI Prompts for Listening

1- "Tell me a short story in simple English. Speak slowly. After finishing, ask me 3 comprehension questions."

2- "Read one sentence at a time for shadowing practice. Wait for me to say 'next' before you continue."

3- "Read a short paragraph for dictation. Pause between each sentence."

4- "Let's have a simple conversation in English about daily life. Use basic vocabulary and correct me if I don't understand."

5- "Tell me the same story three times: once slowly, once at normal speed, once fast."
